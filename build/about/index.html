Hi, I'm Ollie, a designer and front end developer who has just moved to Canada. I've just left Kyan and taking some time off to travel and see the world. I'm married to the beautiful Kat Neville who runs our web design company at thisiscapra with another group of talented of people.

This is a stripped down version of my old site with the focus re-aligned to my work and music rather than a blog as I never manage to keep it up to date. If you came here looking for a blog post I'm afraid they have now all been taken down. The blog might reappear at some point in the future.

Elsewhere on the internet you can follow me on twitter, check out my dj mixes on soundcloud, see what I'm listening to on lastfm or view my photos and older bits of work on flickr.

I am not taking on any projects at the moment, so for any work enquiries please contact Kyan or thisiscapra. For anything else just drop me an email.
